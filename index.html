ABOUT ✦
THE ARTIST BEHIND THE WORK

I am KOE — a digital artist working on Ibis Paint, turning ideas in my head into sketches and finished pieces. I draw characters, cars, landscapes, and different creative concepts, always trying to improve my style and add more detail with each work. For me, drawing is just a way to express what I can't always say in words and keep growing with every piece I make. "Art has no limit."
