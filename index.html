About the Showcase ✦

The Featured Showcase presents a curated selection of artworks chosen for their creativity, quality, and visual impact. Bringing together a variety of styles, characters, and scenes, this collection highlights some of the most notable pieces featured throughout DigiArtz.

Each artwork has been selected for its artistic merit, attention to detail, and overall presentation, offering visitors a refined look at the work displayed within the gallery. Designed to provide an engaging viewing experience, the showcase celebrates digital art through imagination, craftsmanship, and creative expression.

About the Gallery ✦

The Gallery features the latest additions to DigiArtz, showcasing newly uploaded artworks as they become available. From character illustrations and landscapes to creative experiments and detailed compositions, this collection is constantly growing with fresh content.

Visitors can explore recent creations, discover new styles, and stay up to date with the newest artwork featured on the platform. The gallery serves as an evolving archive of digital art, highlighting the most current additions while celebrating creativity and artistic expression.

About the Characters ✦

The Characters collection brings together a diverse range of character-focused artwork, featuring original creations, fan art, game characters, anime-inspired designs, and real-life inspired portraits. Each piece explores unique personalities, styles, and visual storytelling through character illustration.

From iconic fan-favorite characters to completely original concepts, this collection highlights the creativity and artistic interpretation behind every design. Whether inspired by existing worlds or created from imagination, these artworks celebrate the appeal and diversity of character art across different genres and mediums.

ABOUT ✦
THE ARTIST BEHIND THE WORK

I am KOE — a digital artist working on Ibis Paint, turning ideas in my head into sketches and finished pieces. I draw characters, cars, landscapes, and different creative concepts, always trying to improve my style and add more detail with each work. For me, drawing is just a way to express what I can't always say in words and keep growing with every piece I make. "Art has no limit."
